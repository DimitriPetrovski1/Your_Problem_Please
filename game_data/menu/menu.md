You'll encounter three types of scenarios:

EMAILS - Review suspicious emails and identify red flags like fake senders, urgent language, requests for personal information, or offers that seem too good to be true.

MESSAGES - Examine text messages from friends and strangers. Watch for impersonators, suspicious links, urgent demands, and requests for sensitive data.

REAL LIFE - Help your friends make safe decisions about public Wi-Fi, USB drives, QR codes, app downloads, and sharing personal information online.

MINI-GAME: AD BLOCKER
Close pop-up ads before they reach the top of the screen! Click the X button on each ad to earn points. Miss too many and you'll lose lives. The game gets faster as you progress.

EARN & SPEND
Complete challenges to earn money. Visit the shop to customize your character and office with cool items!

TIPS FOR SUCCESS
Read carefully - scammers use clever tricks
Check sender addresses for misspellings
Never share passwords or personal info
When in doubt, don't click!
Stay safe online and have fun learning!
